## Job Description - DevSecOps Engineer for MPS

**Client:** Motherson  
**Title:** DevSecOps Engineer for MPS  
**Location:** Bengaluru, Karnataka, India  
**Experience:** 3–6 Years

## Job Description

### Responsibilities

- **CI/CD Pipelines** — Design, build, and maintain continuous integration and delivery pipelines (Jenkins or GitLab CI).

- **Infrastructure as Code** — Provision and manage cloud infrastructure using tools like Terraform.

- **Cloud Operations** — Manage and optimize workloads on AWS or Azure including compute, networking, storage, and security.

- **Containerization & Orchestration** — Deploy and manage containerized applications using Docker and Kubernetes (EKS or AKS).

- **Monitoring & Observability** — Implement logging, metrics, and alerting using tools like Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, and OpenTelemetry.

- **Security & Compliance** — Embed DevSecOps practices into pipelines; manage secrets, access controls, dependency scanning, and vulnerability scanning (preferably GitLab Security).

- **Client-Facing Support** — Act as a technical point of contact for clients regarding production environment health, deployments, and incident escalations.

- **Collaboration** — Work closely with developers, architects, and product teams to support platform needs and best practices.

### Nice to Have

- Experience with service mesh technologies (Istio)
- Knowledge of GitOps workflows (ArgoCD, Flux)
- Familiarity with FinOps — cloud cost monitoring and optimization
- Experience with database administration (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis)
- Relevant certifications:
  - AWS Solutions Architect
  - CKA (Certified Kubernetes Administrator)
  - HashiCorp Terraform Associate

### Experience Required

- 3–5 years in a DevSecOps, Platform Engineering, or Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) role

### Overall Experience

- 3–6 years
