# Role: Offshore AgentOps & DevSecOps Engineer

**Company:** Intellectyx Data Science India Private Limited
**Department:** Engineering
**Experience:** 5–7 Years
**Employment Type:** Full-Time
**Location:** India

## Overview

Intellectyx is seeking an **Offshore AgentOps & DevSecOps Engineer** to design, secure, deploy, and operate AI agents and cloud-native applications at scale across the company's AI portfolio.

This role combines **Agent Operations (LLM / AI Agents)** with **DevSecOps best practices**, with **AWS** as the primary cloud platform.
